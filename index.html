Greenhouse Project

Building greenhouses offers a sustainable solution to several pressing challenges facing modern agriculture, particularly in the context of escalating issues related to climate change and land management. By efficiently utilizing land space, greenhouses address concerns over the usability of crops amidst rising temperatures, issues stemming from poor space management, higher rate of methane emissions associated with conventional farming methods, and income opportunities for farmers.
